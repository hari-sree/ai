
Parallel Multi-agent
--------------------

1.
Use a fan-out/fan-in (N researchers -> synthesizer) approach to research the question "${}"

Eg : Use a fan-out/fan-in (N researchers -> synthesizer) approach to research the question "What is the best diet approach for someone who is 74 kgs, is 167 cms tall and is skinny fat with a tummy" ?

Minimum 3 sub-agents, use Sonnet for the individual research and Opus to synthesize

2.

Eg: Use stochastic multi-agent consensus to determine all of the different ways that you can make a nice tasting sauce using tomatillos. I want every agent to come up with at least ten independent responses, then have them synthesized and turned into just a giant list of all of the possible things you could do.
